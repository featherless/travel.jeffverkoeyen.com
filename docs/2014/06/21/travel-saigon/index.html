Thanh Guesthouse

Centrally located in the heart of Disrtict 1, Saigon, with great shopping, cafes, bars and music.

  • 84/26 Bui Vien St District 1, Ho Chi Minh
  • 76% on hostelworld
  • 5 out of 5 on tripadvisor

New Sky Hotel

New Sky Hotel is near tourist area so you can easily find good food and fun in a short distance. The friendly staffs will bring to you warmly service and ready to assist you.

  • 114/34 De Tham Street, District 1, Ho Chi Minh
  • No reviews on hostelworld
  • 5 out of 5 on tripadvisor

Eco Backpackers Hostel

Eco Backpackers Hostel prides itself on delivering a budget experience with cleanliness, friendliness and a dash of style built-in.

We are right in the main street of Saigon’s Backpacker area (not in dark alley).

So getting there is just 15 minutes by ground transport from the Airport. Buses from Cambodia and other provinces is only 1 minute walk from our location !

All Saigon’s main attractions are within walking distance !

  • 264 De Tham Street, Pham Ngu Lao ward, Dist.1, Ho Chi Minh
  • 87% on hostelworld
  • 4.5 out of 5 on tripadvisor
  • Really solid reviews all around. Clean, good wifi, nice beds. $6/night dorm beds.

Khoi Hostel

Khoi Hostel located in a safe & quite alley in Back Packer area. Staying here you will feed what is ‘ideally placed for nightlife’.

From the airport you catch a Taxi to Pham Ngu Lao street and stop in Thai Binh Market, you will see the alley next to Thai Binh market, get into the alley 373 about 5 doors, Khoi Hostel in the left hand.

From the airport you catch a bus number 152 and stop in Ben Thanh Market. From Ben Thanh market, follow the park by walking about 15 minutes you will see Thai Binh market, get into the alley 373 next to Thai Binh market about 5 doors, Khoi Hostel in the left hand.

From the Cambodia Bus stop go awalk about 5 minutes you will see Thai Binh market, get into the alley 373 next to Thai Binh market about 5 doors, Khoi Hostel in the left hand.

  • 373/11 Pham Ngu Lao St, Dist 1, Ho Chi Minh
  • 88% on hostelworld
  • 4.5 out of 5 on tripadvisor
  • Even higher rating than Eco Backpackers and they include food for just a $1 more than Eco at $7/night dorm beds.
