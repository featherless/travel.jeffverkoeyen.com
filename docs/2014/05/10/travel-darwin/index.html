Australia: Darwin
10 May 2014

We only spent a day in Darwin while we waited for our connecting flight to Bali. The airport is quite a distance from anywhere and all forms of convenient transportation would’ve cost at least $40 AUD for the day, so we decided to skip exploring Darwin in favour of sleeping at the airport and doing some open source work.
